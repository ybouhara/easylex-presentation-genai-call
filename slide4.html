Key Feasibility Questions

Aligned with Innoviris GENAI Call for Projects

This feasibility study supports the preparation phase of our generative AI project, targeting TRL levels 3-5. We will develop a proof of concept while answering critical technical and business questions.

Technical Questions

Model Selection & Dependence

Which models are most appropriate for trilingual legal information extraction? What level of dependency is acceptable?

Data Sufficiency & Quality

Is our dataset of 25,000 legal documents sufficient, representative, and unbiased?

Business Questions

ROI & Revenue Model

What are the costs and projected revenues? Which pricing model is most appropriate for our target market?

Content Ownership & Liability

How will generated content be treated in terms of ownership or liability?

Study Deliverables

1. Comprehensive feasibility report addressing all technical and business questions

2. Functional proof of concept demonstrating trilingual legal navigation

3. Go/no-go recommendation with implementation roadmap if positive

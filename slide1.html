Key Challenges

1

Volume & Complexity

Record-breaking volume of legal content (144K+ pages/year) with complex multi-level governance interactions.

2

Fragmentation & Access

581 municipalities with separate publications, often as non-searchable PDFs with no central repository.

3

Jurisdictional Uncertainty

Overlapping competencies between governance levels, leading to conflicts and interpretation challenges.

4

Multilingual Barriers

Separate FR/NL/DE versions with no cross-referencing, requiring multiple searches in different systems.

Feasibility Study Focus: Creating an AI intellectual exoskeleton that reduces legal research time by 30-50% while keeping the professional's strategic analysis at the heart of every decision.
